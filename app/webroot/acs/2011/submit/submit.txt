# Paper Submission

Please fill out the form below to submit your paper to the symposium 
for review. Make sure that your submission conforms to the 
[AAAI two-column format](http://www.aaai.org/Publications/Author/) 
and is no more than eight (8) pages in length. We will return without
review any paper that diverges from this format or that exceeds this 
length.
